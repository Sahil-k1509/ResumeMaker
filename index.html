Instructions & Tips

  • Even though all sections have 4 points initially, please try to fit the information in only 1-2 points. Remember, A short and on point resume is better than long resume.
  • In any case, try not to exceed one page. A longer resume is most likely to get rejected as recruiters won't have time to go through all of it. Keep it short and reserve details for interview.
  • This is a template resume. You can edit the content and download the resume in pdf format.
  • The sections on left side are reorderable. Drag and drop to change position.
  • Education section has switch button that switches to tabular format if you prefer it.
  • Enter the url for your website(github/portfolio) in the input box and the link will redirect you to that link even in pdf format.
